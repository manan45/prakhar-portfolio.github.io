'Not An Ad' campaign

Instead of writing outright pitches, I offered our users some friendly reminders. This broke the clutter of monotonous ads along with piquing the interest of the viewer, making them think what's on the other side.

With the highest CTR of 3.1 and average bounce rate of 46%, this series became the most successful display campaign at GeeksforGeeks.
